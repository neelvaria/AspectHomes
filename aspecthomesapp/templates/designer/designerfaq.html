FaQ's.

  • How do I request access to client information and project details?

    Access to client information and project details can be requested by submitting a formal request through our designated platform or by contacting the administrative team directly. Upon receiving your request, we will provide you with access to the necessary resources..

  • Who should I contact if I encounter technical difficulties with software?

    If you encounter technical difficulties with project management tools or software, please reach out to our IT support team for assistance. You can submit a support ticket through our IT helpdesk portal or contact the IT support hotline for immediate assistance..

  • How are project timelines and deadlines communicated to clients?

    Project timelines and deadlines are communicated to clients through formal project proposals and contracts. Once a project scope has been finalized, our administrative team will work with you to establish project timelines and deadlines, which will then be communicated to the client for approval.

  • How do you handle unforeseen challenges or changes during the project?

    We understand that unforeseen challenges and changes may arise during the course of a project. We approach these situations with flexibility and professionalism, keeping you informed every step of the way. We will work together to find solutions that meet your needs and minimize any disruptions to the project timeline.

  • What are your payment terms and accepted payment methods?

    Our payment terms vary depending on the scope of the project and the client's preferences. We typically require a deposit upfront to secure our services, with the remaining balance due upon completion of the project. We accept various payment methods.

  • What types of interior design services do you offer?

    We offer a wide range of interior design services tailored to meet our clients' needs. These services include residential interior design, commercial interior design, space planning, furniture selection, lighting design, color consultations, and project management.
